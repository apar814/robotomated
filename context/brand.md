# Robotomated — Brand

## Name & Tagline
Robotomated — "The Intelligence Layer for the Robotics Era"
"Your warehouse, robotomated." "Your facility, robotomated."
"Your home, robotomated." Past tense = the job is done.

## Domain Strategy
robotomated.com — primary brand, main platform
robotomate.com — redirects, campaign URLs ("go.robotomate.com")
robotomato.com — parked. Future consumer sub-brand or merch.

## Design Principles
Trust first: every claim sourced, every score explainable, no black boxes
Complexity on demand: simple default view, deep data on click
AI as assistant, not gatekeeper: always browsable without AI
Mobile-native: 60%+ discovery is mobile, every feature works on phone
Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA minimum, vocabulary explained on hover

## Tone
"Bridges the gap between techy-and-intimidating and
shallow-and-marketing-driven. Confidence of Jony Ive's product design,
trust of Consumer Reports editorial."

Authoritative but warm. Expert without being condescending.
Write for the foreman, not the PhD — but don't dumb it down for either.

GOOD:
"The Roomba J9+ navigates tight spaces better than any robot we've
tested. But at $799, you're paying a premium for features most homes
don't need. The Roborock Q7 Max scores within 3 points at half the price."

BAD:
"This robot utilizes advanced SLAM-based navigation algorithms with
LiDAR-enabled environmental mapping for optimal path planning."
(Save this for the developer docs, not the review.)

## Editorial Independence
THE moat. Non-negotiable.
- Review methodology is public
- Scoring is explainable
- "We were wrong" moments published loudly
- Never accept money to influence a review score
- Manufacturers cannot buy placement in recommendations
- Sponsored content clearly labeled, never disguised as editorial

## Photography / Visual Content
Product: clean, well-lit, lifestyle context (robot in a real kitchen,
not a white void). Show scale — person next to robot.
Reviews: real testing footage, comparison shots, close-up details.
Data viz: clean charts for score breakdowns, price history, comparisons.
