# Robotomated — Portfolio Connections

## Brand Group: AI/Tech Group
Alongside Schedg.ai (now Zirqul), FiWise (now Zaura)
Robotomated is the content + commerce + AI play in the tech group.

## Cross-Company Connections
- **Kleen.ai**: Kleen scores products. Robotomated scores robots.
  Same "trust through transparency" model. Same scoring philosophy
  (public methodology, explainable, editorial independence). Could
  share architectural patterns (scoring engine, review system, SEO).
- **BuildTal**: construction industry crossover. Construction robots
  (Dusty Robotics, Built Robotics, Hilti Jaibot) are a high-value
  vertical for Robotomated. BuildTal's AEC audience = potential
  Robotomated enterprise customers.
- **KindrNature**: no direct connection.
- **Zaura**: no direct connection.
- **Zirqul**: no direct connection.

## Unique Portfolio Position
Robotomated is the only MEDIA + MARKETPLACE business in the portfolio.
Revenue model is content-driven (affiliate, subscriptions, data)
rather than SaaS or ecommerce. Different operational DNA — more like
The Verge or Wirecutter than a software company.

## Shared Patterns
Scoring engines (Kleen + Robotomated) could share architectural DNA.
Both use: public methodology, weighted dimensions, letter/numeric grades,
AI for explanations (not scoring), editorial independence as moat.
