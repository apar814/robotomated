JetBrains Mono — table data only
LAYOUT
Sidebar + main content. Tables over cards. Scannable rows. Filter-heavy.
ANIMATION
Sparkline micro-charts. Pulse indicators for live data. Minimal motion — function over flourish.
FEEL
Opening Linear and immediately seeing your work. No hero banners. No marketing fluff. Just the data you came for, filterable, sortable, instant.
Risk: May feel too utilitarian for a site that also needs to attract and convert. Less visual wow factor. The homepage becomes the browse page — could work, but loses the "platform brand" moment.
