# Product Hunt Submission — Robotomated

## Tagline (60 chars)
Find the right robot for any job — guided by AI.

## Description (260 chars)
Robotomated is the intelligence layer for the robotics era. Browse 500+ robots, compare transparent RoboScores across 8 dimensions, and get AI-powered recommendations from Claude. Free to use. No black-box ratings. From $200 vacuums to $2.5M surgical systems.

## First Comment (Founder Story)

Hey Product Hunt! I'm the founder of Robotomated.

The idea started when I was helping a friend find a robot vacuum. We spent 3 hours reading reviews across 6 different sites, comparing specs in spreadsheets, and still weren't sure which one to buy. Then I realized — if it's this hard for a $500 vacuum, imagine buying a $50,000 warehouse robot.

The robotics market is exploding but there's no single place to research, compare, and get honest guidance across every category. So we built one.

What makes us different:

**RoboScore** — Every robot gets a transparent 0-100 score across 8 weighted dimensions. The methodology is fully public. Manufacturers can't pay to influence scores.

**AI Advisor** — Powered by Claude. Describe your needs in plain English, get 3 specific recommendations with prices and scores. No hallucinated products — only robots verified in our database.

We're a small team, bootstrapped, and obsessed with editorial independence. We'd love your feedback on the experience and which features to build next.

Try the AI Advisor first — it's the fastest way to see what we do: robotomated.com/advisor

## 5 Key Features

1. **Transparent RoboScore** — 8-dimension scoring system with public methodology. No pay-to-play.
2. **AI Advisor powered by Claude** — Describe your needs, get 3 matched recommendations with prices and scores.
3. **Every category covered** — Consumer, warehouse, manufacturing, healthcare, delivery, and construction robots.
4. **Head-to-head comparisons** — Side-by-side specs, scores, and pricing across any two robots.
5. **Price tracking & alerts** — Monitor prices across retailers. Get emailed when a robot drops below your target.
